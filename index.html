About

Civil Engineer.
Climate Thinker.
Water Dreamer.

"The river knows where it's going. My job is to listen."

I'm Somnath Luitel — a civil engineer with a deep fascination for water, earth science, and the application of artificial intelligence to understand our changing planet.

I founded Paani AI, an initiative that weds data science with the ancient urgency of water — building flood forecasting tools, water quality models, and climate-resilient systems for Nepal and beyond.

My published research spans flood hazard mapping at the settlement scale and InSAR-based land subsidence monitoring in Pokhara Valley. When I'm not in the lab or field, you'll find me deep in a book, on a mountain trail, or writing in Nepali about things that matter.
