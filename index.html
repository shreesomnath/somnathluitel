About Me

I am a passionate civil engineer focused on water resource management and environmental sustainability.

Founder of Paani AI, an initiative using data science to tackle water challenges. Always looking for new opportunities to apply engineering for a better tomorrow.

I'm from Morang, Nepal, part of the Terai region often called the Golden Land because of its fertile soil and deep connection to agriculture. Growing up, I saw how vital water is for farming and daily life, and how often it's poorly managed. That's where my dream started—to do something real and useful in Nepal's water sector.

I also love to travel, write, and spend time with people, listening to their stories and learning from their lives.
