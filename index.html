About Me

I am a passionate civil engineer focused on water resource management and environmental sustainability. Currently working on satellite-based precipitation research and bias correction in Nepal with international collaboration.

Founder of Paani AI, an initiative using data science to tackle water challenges. Always looking for new opportunities to apply engineering for a better tomorrow.
